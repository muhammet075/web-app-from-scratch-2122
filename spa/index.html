CryptoAbi helps you with all your crypto solutions. You can easly set a alert push-up on a specifik price on each crypto coin which you choose. We will always send you a notification message when the price of a coin hits the price of your wish..

How it works?

Easy! Click on the search icon on top of the app. Write your favorite coin and click on the search button. Then click on yout coin on the search results. Then you will see the current price, market cap, change and the last update time. If you like it, you can add the coin on your favorite list. Press on the closing button, on the top. Then press on the bell icon, on the corner.
